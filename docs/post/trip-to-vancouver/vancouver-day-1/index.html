Day 1 of Vancouver Trip

August 25, 2022 writing, Vancouver, photography, tourist

The first day of my trip to Vancouver was super exciting. I first took a flight from Ontario to Vancouver and landed in Vancouver at around 11:00 PST.

Once I checked into my hotel and got ready, I took the subway to downtown Vancouver. When I was in Downtown Vancouver, I went to a town called Gastown. In Gastown, a Steam clock would ring every 15 minutes. After this, I took a Seabus to North Vancouver, another Island. When I arrived, there were many small shops and restaurants. Benches and tables were set up at a harbor for people to sit and enjoy the view of the buildings in Vancouver city. I explored a bit of North Vancouver for a bit more and then left for my hotel.
